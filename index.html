What are the Toronto Sports Teams?

There are 4 major sports teams in Toronto that are in respective major leagues in different sports. Those said teams are the Toronto Maple Leafs of the National Hockey League, the Toronto Raptors of the National Basketball Association and the Toronto Blue Jays of Major League Baseball. The Toronto-based Major League Soccer team, Toronto FC, is known to some extent in Toronto and is supported but the team isn't as popular as the ones mentioned above.

The Success of Toronto Sports teams

These Toronto sports teams have a history of winning, but very few of them have a recent history of winning.

In terms of recent winning history, the Toronto Raptors have claimed that category. In 2019, the Toronto Raptors were crowned the champions of the NBA. Other than that, the only recently successful Toronto sports teams have been the Toronto Blue Jays, Toronto FC, and the Toronto Argonauts.
